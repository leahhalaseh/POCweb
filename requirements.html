Requirements

Specified by the client

Must have

  1. The ability for the Head Office to monitor all Field Engineers's 'stress levels' (based primarily on heart rate) in an integrated dashboard.
  2. The ability for the Head Office to send alerts to Field Engineers.
  3. The ability for Field Engineers to respond to alerts.
  4. The ability for Field Engineers to raise an alarm manually or automatically with a 'panic button'.

Should have

  1. Display Heart rate and skin temperature readings in phone app (including warnings if it goes above/below certain values) and integration to panic button.
  2. GPS integration to panic button to aid Head Office to respond to emergencies (may need to have an on/off function if trade unions object).
  3. Track steps or distance travelled to measure whether the worker is overworked, and integrate to panic button.

Could have

  1. Display warnings based on stress levels (e.g. sudden drops) on a traffic light system.
  2. Measure and display 'mood colours' based on personalised heart rate comparison and stress levels.
  3. On site - Off site button to replace the call lone workers do every 30min and track how long the worker has been working.
